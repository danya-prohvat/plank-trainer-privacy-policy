Privacy Policy for Plank Trainer

Effective Date: December 30, 2024

Last Updated: December 30, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Plank Trainer ("we," "our," or "the app"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application collects, uses, and protects your information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Stored Locally on Your Device

Plank Trainer stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Workout History: Records of your completed plank exercises, including duration, date, and time
  • Custom Training Plans: Your personalized workout routines and training programs that you create
  • App Settings: Your preferences for notifications, sound, and other app configurations
  • Progress Statistics: Your workout statistics and achievement data

Important: All this data is stored only on your device. We do not have access to this information, and it is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

2.2 Push Notification Data

If you enable push notifications, we use the device's notification system to send you workout reminders. We only store notification preferences locally on your device. No personal information is collected or transmitted for notification purposes.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Photos, videos, or other media files
  • Contact lists
  • Health data beyond what you manually input for workouts
  • Analytics or usage tracking data

3. How We Use Your Information

The data stored locally on your device is used solely to:

  • Display your workout history and progress
  • Enable you to create and manage custom training plans
  • Provide workout reminders through push notifications (if enabled)
  • Enhance your user experience within the app

4. Data Storage and Security

All your data is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms provided by your mobile operating system (Android/iOS). Since we do not collect or transmit your data to external servers, your information remains under your control at all times.

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to any third parties. The app does not integrate with any third-party analytics services, advertising networks, or data collection tools.

6. Push Notifications

If you opt-in to receive push notifications:

  • Notifications are delivered through your device's operating system notification service (Google Firebase for Android, Apple Push Notification Service for iOS)
  • You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings or within the app
  • We do not track or collect data about your notification interactions

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used our app, please contact us.

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is accessible within the app
  • Delete: You can delete your workout history and custom plans within the app, or uninstall the app to remove all data
  • Modify: You can edit your custom training plans and settings at any time
  • Opt-out: You can disable push notifications in the app settings or your device settings

9. Data Retention

Your data is retained on your device until you choose to delete it manually or uninstall the app. When you uninstall the app, all locally stored data is automatically removed from your device.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Posting a notice in the app (for significant changes)

Your continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

11. International Users

Since all data is stored locally on your device and we do not collect or transfer any data, there are no international data transfer concerns.

12. Disclaimer

Plank Trainer is a fitness application intended to assist with exercise routines. It is not a substitute for professional medical advice. Please consult with a healthcare provider before beginning any exercise program. We are not responsible for any injuries or health issues that may occur from using this app.
